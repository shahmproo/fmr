Step Into

A New Smile Experience

At Chicago FMR, we offer a range of advanced dental services to help you achieve a healthy, beautiful smile. Our Full Mouth Rehabilitation services are designed to restore or replace all of your teeth, whether through crowns, bridges, or implants, to improve both the function and appearance of your smile. For those seeking a discreet way to straighten their teeth, our Invisalign treatment provides custom clear aligners that gradually shift your teeth into the desired position without the need for traditional braces. However, if more complex alignment issues are present, our Braces options—whether metal or ceramic—offer a reliable and effective solution to correct misalignments and bite problems. Finally, for patients with missing teeth, our Single Implants provide a durable and natural-looking replacement, using a titanium post that integrates with your jawbone and a custom crown to restore both function and aesthetics. Whatever your needs, we’re committed to providing you with personalized, state-of-the-art care to ensure your smile is healthy and radiant.

Single dental implants are a modern and effective solution for replacing a single missing tooth. Unlike traditional bridges that require support from adjacent teeth, dental implants are standalone restorations that mimic the structure and function of natural teeth.
