Permanent Zirconia Implants & PMMA Teeth

Permanent zirconia implants and PMMA teeth are advanced dental solutions designed to replace missing teeth. Zirconia implants provide durable support for fixed dentures with 4-6 implants placed in the jaw, ensuring stability and function. PMMA teeth, made from strong, biocompatible material, offer a natural-looking, long-lasting restoration. Both options are designed to enhance aesthetics and functionality, giving patients a stable and beautiful smile.

The Treatment Process

Evaluation and Planning

Begin with a comprehensive oral examination using advanced imaging (e.g., Cone-Beam CT or 3D scanning) to assess bone health and determine treatment eligibility

Preparation and Foundation

Perform necessary extractions, bone grafting (if needed), and preparation of natural teeth or implants to create a stable base for restorations.

Customization and Placement

Fabricate custom zirconia or PMMA prosthetics using CAD/CAM technology for precision and aesthetics, followed by secure placement on implants or prepared teeth.

Visualization and Follow-Up

Offer pre-surgery 3D visualizations of the final smile and provide detailed post-care instructions with follow-up appointments to ensure long-term success.
